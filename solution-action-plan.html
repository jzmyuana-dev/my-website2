Campaign Demand

Invest in healing, transition, families, youth, and equality.

What this demand includes

  • Build basic socioeconomic equality through housing, income security, health care, education, employment, and community infrastructure.
  • Improve access to cultural and spiritual programming for Indigenous people in custody and in community-based alternatives.
  • Provide more resources to support the transition from prison back into community, including release planning, housing, identification, health care, and cultural connection.
  • Create more programming that meets the needs of women in prison, especially Indigenous women affected by violence, family separation, trauma, and criminalization.
  • Support children whose parent is incarcerated so imprisonment does not deepen family separation and intergenerational harm.
  • Help youth reintegrate into community through school, mentorship, mental health care, cultural programming, family support, and safe housing.
  • Fund prevention before incarceration, not only services after people have already entered prison.
